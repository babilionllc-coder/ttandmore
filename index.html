Common Questions

FAQ

How can I find you at the airport?
Once you pass through customs and immigration, head to the main exit. You will likely be approached by timeshare vendors offering help with tours and transfers — please ignore them and continue walking. Look for our staff in the airport arrivals area; they will be holding a TT&MORE sign.
Do we have to share the ride with other people?
No. All of our transfers are private.
What happens if our flight is delayed?
It is very important that you notify us of any changes to your flight. Our staff will be monitoring your flight and will be waiting for you upon arrival.
What type of vehicles are used?
Toyota Hiace, Nissan Urvan (capacity: up to 10 passengers) and Toyota Sienna, Toyota Highlander (capacity: up to 4 passengers with limited luggage).
Do you provide car seats for children?
Yes, we can provide car seats in our vehicles. However, our staff is not authorized to install them; installation must be done by the client due to insurance regulations. Car seats are available upon request — please specify this in your reservation.
What currency can we use to pay?
Our prices are in USD, but through PayPal you can pay in almost any currency. If paying in cash, we accept USD and MXN.
Can we make a stop on the way to the hotel?
Yes, stops are allowed with prior notice. For a stop of up to 1 hour, our fee is $30 USD. We recommend informing us after making your reservation if you will need to make a stop.
Is my reservation refundable?
You may cancel your reservation at least 24 hours before the service with a 7% fee for banking charges. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made less than 24 hours before the service.
Are pets allowed in transfer services?
We are pet-friendly! We can transport domestic pets as long as they are in their carriers, and the carrier must fit within the vehicle.

Mission

Our mission at TT & More is to provide a transportation service that exceeds our passengers' expectations. We focus on delivering an efficient, reliable, hygienic, and comfortable service while ensuring safety and punctuality at all times.

Our team of trained professionals works tirelessly to provide exceptional service, continuously striving for improvement and customer satisfaction, allowing us to create unforgettable experiences.

Our Journey

In 1981, when I arrived in Cancún from Mexico City, I began working at a hotel as a security guard. Later, I worked as a taxi driver, and during that time I met people who worked at the airport and transitioned into becoming a tourist driver.

Over time, I envisioned the first tourist transportation companies and asked myself… would it be possible to have my own transportation company? Without overthinking it and with a lot of effort, I created my own tourist transportation company in 1993.

Now, with 33 years of experience, we are embarking on this new stage in the digital industry with our own website, providing the same quality of service, friendliness, and a 100% commitment to our valued clients.
